# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:**  12.07.2025

## Introduction

Hair Try-On Service ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Instagram messaging service.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Images:** When you send images through Instagram Direct Messages, we temporarily store:
  - Your portrait photo
  - Hairstyle reference photo
  - The processed/merged result image

### Automatically Collected Data
- Instagram User ID (for message delivery)
- Message timestamps
- Technical data (IP address, device information)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Process your images using AI technology (OpenAI API)
- Generate merged hairstyle images
- Send results back to you via Instagram Direct Messages
- Improve our service quality

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **OpenAI API:** For AI-powered image processing
  - Your images are sent to OpenAI for processing
  - OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
- **Instagram/Meta:** For messaging functionality
  - Instagram's privacy policy: https://help.instagram.com/519522125107875

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Temporary Storage:** Images are stored temporarily (up to 24 hours) for processing
- **Output Images:** Processed images are stored for 7 days, then automatically deleted
- **Logs:** Technical logs are retained for 30 days for troubleshooting purposes

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Encrypted data transmission
- Limited access to stored data
- Automatic data deletion

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request access to your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of data collection (by not using the service)

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
